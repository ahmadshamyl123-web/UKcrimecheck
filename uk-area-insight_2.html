Search a UK postcode, place, or coordinate to see the local crime picture and community profile.
Crime data covers England, Wales & Northern Ireland and shows offences reported to police within roughly a 1-mile radius; locations are snapped to anonymised map points by the police, so they are approximate. Community figures are from the ONS 2021 Census at Local Authority level (England & Wales) and describe the wider area, not a single street. This tool is for general information only.
